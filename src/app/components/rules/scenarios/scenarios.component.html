Scenarios

A 'Scenario card' comes in three types, The 'Battlefield' cards, the "Spoils of War Objective" and the 'Denizens of the Land' cards. Each player should have selected 2 of each type of card for a total of 6 cards.

Battlefield

The scenario card provides a detailed description of what type and where the terrain elements for the battlefield are setup. Along with the terrain, this card also details the location of any other fixed elements on the map such as player encampments and resources elements This card also details the escalation level for the battle, that means the amount of requisition points each player has available to build their forces with and also how many reinforcements they receives at the start of each round after the first.

Primary Objective

This card details which fixed objectives are in play and their special rules

Spoils of War Objective

This card describes any objectives that must be retrieved from the battlefield and either held during the battle or brought back to your encampment

Denizens of the Land

This card describes the strengths, numbers, placement and potential rewards of the neutral monsters to be placed at the neutral monster emplacements.
