Key Concepts

Resources

Command Points

Command Points (CPs) are used to command your forces and direct them in battle. CPs are replenished at the start of every round, but at the same time, an upkeep must be paid in CPs depending on the number of units under your control. Additional CPs can be gained from various scenario elements and units.

Requisition Points

Requisition Points are used to muster new forces to your army. This can be done as your action during a turn and the unit will usually be placed next to your encampment.

Mana Points

Mana Points are used for casting, countering and dispelling spells. Mana points are replenished at the start of every round and can be gained via various scenario elements and some special units

Encampment

Your encampment is the location of your general, if this is destroyed, you will usually lose the game, but this can change dependant on the scenario played.
