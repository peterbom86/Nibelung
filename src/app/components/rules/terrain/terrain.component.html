Terrain

Forest

Blocks line of sight. Units only partially inside the forest can be seen (and by then, targeted by ranged attacks). Infantry units moving across suffers -1” movement, cavalry units suffer -2” movement. Units within a forest gains +2/+2/+0 defence except if they have charged during the preceding turn.

Deep Lake

Blocks movement for all non-flying models

Swamp

Caps movement at 3” max for all non-flying models

Units inside a swamp suffers -2/-2/-1 defence and -1-/0 attack

Hills

Blocks line-of-sight to non-flying units behind the hill.

Units on the hill gain +1/+2/+0 defence and +1/+0 attack. Non-flying ranged units also gain +4” range.

Non-Flying charging units starting their charge completely on the hill also gain +2” movement if they end their movement no longer completely on the hill.

Rocky ground

Non-flying charge movements across rocky ground suffers -2” movement.

Fog

Blocks line of sight

Units within a fog gain +0/+2/+0 defence. (This is mostly applicable to units not completely within the fog, so that LOS still can be drawn)

Flying units are capped at 3" movement while any part of their movement goes through fog

Notes about line of sight

Units completely within forests or fog cannot see enemy units as well for example for ranged attacks

In order to have line-of-sight, a line must be able to be drawn that does not pass though any LOS blocking terrain.
