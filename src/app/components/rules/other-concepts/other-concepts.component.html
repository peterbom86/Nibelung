Other Concepts

Exchanging resources

Excess Command Points (CP) can be exchanged at the end of each round; 2 CP for one Req. point or 1 CP for 1 XP.

During the turn, Req. points can be exchanged for CPs, one Req. point for one CP.

Controlling objectives

In order to 'Control' an objective, you need to be the only player having a non-fleeing unit within 2" of the objective when it is scored.
