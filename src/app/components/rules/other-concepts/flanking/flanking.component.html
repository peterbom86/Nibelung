During the combat phase, if two models are placed so that they can draw a line from their centerpoints through an enemy unit, the models are said to be flanking the enemy unit.

When flanking occurs, the last unit that moved into contact with the enemy (the flanking unit) will get a flanking token. While having the flanking token, the unit gets the flanking bonus of its stat card.

If a unit is both charging and receiving the flanking bonus, take the highest number of both effects for both the dice rolled and dice picked (eg. if the two bonuses are +1/+0 and +0/+1, the final result would be +1/+1)
