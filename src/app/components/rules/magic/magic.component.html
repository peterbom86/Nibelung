Magic

During a players initiative, he or she may, either before or after activating a unit, elect to cast a spell

To cast a spell, the player selects the spell from his mages available spells, selects an eligble target and chooses how much mana he wants to spend on the spell, then his opponent can decide if he wants to counter the spell or not.

After the spell is either resolved or countered the player can continue the turn.
