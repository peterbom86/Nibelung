Round Description

A round consists of three turns.

At the start of every round you do the following

  1. Replenish Command Points, Mana Points and Requisition Points.
  2. Pay upkeep (in CP) for the units you currently have under your control
  3. Determine who will have the initiative in the first turn

At the end of every round, you do the following

  1. Score victory points from objectives according to the scenario
  2. Check if the game has ended (either if there's a winner by being ahead in VP or by having completed round 7)
  3. Respawn neutral units and objectives, according to scenario.
  4. Begin a new round
