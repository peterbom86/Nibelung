Comabt Orders

These orders can be given to units even if they already have recieved a normal order.

Combat orders affects the units combat performance in the ensuing combat phase and are then discarded.

Order Name Cost Effect
Press the attack 1 CP

Makes the unit fight harder and more reckless, gives a bonus to damage and a push ability, but at the cost of defensive capability.

The unit can select a damage track one level above what was rolled and in addition gets a 1” push effect in addition to any other effects. The units suffers -1/-1/-0 defence this phase.

Give ground 1 CP

Makes the unit fight defensively, increasing defence, but lowering damage output and slowly retreats away from the enemy, giving them a chance to follow up.

The unit gets -0/-1 attack and deals -1 damage, its defence is increased by +2/+0/+0 and the enemy unit may push it 1”.

Regroup 1 CP Move a flank token from one unit this model is engaged with to another.
Hold Ground 1 CP Makes the unit hold its ground, at the cost of offence, but with no added defence The units gets -0/-1 attack, but is immune to the effects of push for this phase.
Retreat 1 CP

Makes the unit give ground, lowering both defence and offence, but makes the unit disengage and flee after the round of fighting.

The unit gets -0/-2 attack and deals -1 damage, Its defence is decreased by -2/-2/-0, but at the end of the phase, the unit can make an advance away from the closest enemy model.
